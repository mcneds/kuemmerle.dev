    Profile

    I am an engineering student at UBC driven by one central question: how do you turn complexity into something useful? I am most energized by work with visible impact, including tooling that removes friction for teams, sustainable mechanical systems, and simulation frameworks that improve decisions before build.

    I work best at the overlap of mechanical design, software, and real-world prototyping. My approach is to define constraints clearly, iterate quickly, and build systems that solve problems repeatedly, not just once.

    Selected Projects

    Leaf Walker: A Better Forestry Platform

    Problem: Reduce ground impact and improve maneuverability in constrained forestry pathing.

    Solution: Developed a virtual pivot steering architecture and bogey lift mechanism, with prototype validation and communication-ready engineering documentation.

    Mechanical engineering, steering geometry, sustainability, iterative design

    Tie Rod Tab (UBC Baja SAE)

    Problem: Design and manufacture a compact steering linkage mount with reliable load paths.

    Solution: Delivered a competition-ready tab with manufacture focused geometry and documentation for seamless integration into the broader steering package.

    Automotive design, CAD workflow, GD&T awareness, design review

    SOLIDWORKS 3D Sketch Exporter and 2D/3D Previewer

    Problem: Existing workflows were too slow for high-iteration CAD-to-simulation export cycles.

    Solution: Built a C# add-in with multi-select export, construction filtering, axis remapping, and destination controls, plus a lightweight previewer for rapid validation.

    C#, SOLIDWORKS API, tool architecture, automation

    Walk in the Parkour Pathfinding Demo

    Problem: Traditional random walk and A* approaches produced platforming routes that were technically valid but not engaging.

    Solution: Modeled jump constraints and sampled state space to generate procedural voxel routes with improved feasibility and player experience.

    Algorithm design, systems simulation, C++, Java, Python
