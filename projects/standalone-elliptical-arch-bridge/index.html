Elliptical Arch Popsicle Bridge

A first-year constrained structural design project: build a high-performing bridge with a strict 100-stick, no-modification rule.

Elliptical Arch + Howe Truss Concept

Detailed project page

Problem Definition

The design had to maximize stiffness and load capacity under a hard material constraint: exactly 100 popsicle sticks with no cutting or shaping.

Problem Solution

An elliptical arch with Howe-style triangulation was iterated through trace studies and build trials, then finalized around predictable load paths and buildability.
